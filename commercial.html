Commercial Uses

Unmanned Aerial Systems are widely used in the commercial world for numerous purposes. Photographers and paparazzi have begun using UAS to take photos and videos to sell. Wedding photographers especially have begun using UAS to get better, more picturesque shots for their clients. Even in professional cinematic photography, many photographers are beginning to use UAS to achieve their desired shot with ease. Some contracting firms use UAS to help get a better view of land for surveying. These same firms also use UAS to get a more accurate and reliable survey of factories and other large buildings.
