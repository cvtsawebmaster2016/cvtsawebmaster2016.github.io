ADVISOR

Jason Kofmehl


Mr. Jason Kofmehl is the advisor for Cumberland Valley High School's TSA Chapter. He oversees the chapter and keeps things running smoothly for the officers and members. This is Mr. Kofmehl's third year teaching at Cumberland Valley. He teaches a wide variety of courses, including but not limited to, Principles of Engineering, Digital Electronics, and TV Production. He graduated from California University of Pennsylvania with his Bachelor's Degree of Science in Technology Education in December of 2009. Mr. Kofmehl later returned to California University of Pennsylvania to complete his Master's Degree in Technology Education, and graduated in August of 2013. In his free time, Mr.Kofmehl enjoys Olympic Weightlifting and playing Xbox.
