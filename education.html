Educational Uses

The induction of Unmanned Aerial Systems into education systems has proven itself beneficial to all parties involved. Teachers around the world have found merit in using UAS in their lessons, incorporating them from English to Science. Not only are UAS effective teaching tools, they also successfully keep students interested and engaged in the lesson. Film students use UAS as an alternate way to create a steady, mobile shot, while younger elementary school students use them to get a better idea of how big the world around them truly is. Students of all ages are using UAS to greatly enrich their educational experience.
